TUITIONS

All international students are required to pay a student fee /tuition fee during their registration at the University in accordance with the relevant regulations of the Council of Higher Education, Turkey. The payments should do to the Vakıfbank branch. You may see the location of Vakıfbank branch in Ayazağa Campus, here.

You can reach the fees determined for the academic year 2023-2024 by clicking here.
