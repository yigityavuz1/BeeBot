Announcement about TOEFL , IBT and PTE Exams

* In accordance with the decision of University Senate No: 808 on 27.07.2022, TOEFL IBT Special Home Edition exam results will not be accepted as of the Spring Semester of the 2022-2023 Academic Year.

** In accordance with decision of the University Senate no: 838 on 06.07.2023 TOEFL IBT and PTE Academic exam results must be taken from the exam centers in state universities as of the 2023-2024 Academic Year Fall Semester. Score Reports whose validity have not expired for 2 years will be accepted for students who have been accepted to the university before this period and have been registered for certain.
