2024 - 2025 Minor Application Information and Quotas
----------------------------------------------------

In accordance with the provisions of the Regulation on the Principles of Transfer, Double Major, Minor and Inter-Institutional Credit Transfer between Associate and Undergraduate Degree Programs in Higher Education Institutions and "ITU Undergraduate Programs, Transfer, Double Major Program and Minor Program Directive", qualifications, application dates, allocated quotas and other issues for students who will apply Minor Programs are listed below.

1.  **Conditions**
The candidate must meet the conditions specified both in the [ITU Undergraduate Programs Directive for Transfer](https://www.sis.itu.edu.tr/TR/mevzuat/yatay-cap-yandal-yonerge.php), Double Major Program and Minor Programs and in the application announcement.

3.  **Application Dates:** 12 August 2024 09:00 - 23 August 2024 17:00
Applications must be made from the [the Old Student Information System (Banner)](http://uzay.sis.itu.edu.tr/login/index.php) until 23 August 2024 17:00 for the quotas determined for the Double Major programs. No applications or preferences can be made after 23 August 2024, 17:00.
