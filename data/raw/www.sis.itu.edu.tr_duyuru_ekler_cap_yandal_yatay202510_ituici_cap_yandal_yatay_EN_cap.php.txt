2024-2025 ACADEMIC YEAR DOUBLE MAJOR PROGRAM APPLICATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------

In accordance with the provisions of the Regulation on the Principles of Transfer, Double Major, Minor and Inter-Institutional Credit Transfer between Associate and Undergraduate Degree Programs in Higher Education Institutions and "ITU Undergraduate Programs, Transfer, Double Major Program and Minor Program Directive" , qualifications, application date, allocated quotas and other issues for students applying Double Major Programs are listed below.

1.  **Conditions**
[The candidate must meet the conditions specified both](https://www.sis.itu.edu.tr/TR/mevzuat/yatay-cap-yandal-yonerge.php) in the ITU Undergraduate Programs Directive for Transfer, Double Major Program and Minor Programs and in the application announcement.

3.  **Application Dates:** 12 August 2024 09:00 - 23 August 2024 17:00
Applications must be made from [the Old Student Information System (Banner)](http://uzay.sis.itu.edu.tr/login/index.php) until 23 August 2024 17:00 for the quotas determined for the Double Major programs. No applications or preferences can be made after 23 August 2024, 17:00.
