**Title:** GMI 464E Deck Machinery

**Published Time:** 2022-05-15

**Course Name:** Deck Machinery  
**Course Code:** GMI 464E  
**Semester:** 5-7-8  
**Local Credits:** 2  
**ECTS Credits:** 2  
**Course Language:** English  
**Course Type:** Elective  
**Course Prerequisites:** None  

**Course Description:**  
- Deck crane and davits.  
- Capstan and windlass.  
- Steering gear.  
- Hatch covers.  
- Ramps, covers, and vehicle lifts on RO-RO ships.  
- Bow thruster.  
- Cargo pumps and types.  
- Cargo pumps and components in LNG, LPG, chemical, and oil tankers.  
- Pump applications on tankers.  
- Efficiency and power on pumps.  
- Maintenance, troubles, and remedies of cargo pumps.  

**Course Objectives:**  
1. To teach about the working principles of deck machinery.  
2. To teach about the working principles of RO-RO ships’ systems.  
3. To teach about the working principles of cargo handling equipment.  

**Course Learning Outcomes:**  
Students who pass the course will be able to:  
I. Understand the working principles of deck crane, davits, and capstan-windlass.  
II. Understand the working principles of steering gear.  
III. Know about hatch covers and types.  
IV. Understand the working principles of systems on RO-RO ships and bow thruster.  
V. Understand the working principles of cargo handling pumps.  

**Course Plan:**  
1. Deck cranes  
2. Davits  
3. Capstan and windlass  
4. Steering gears  
5. Steering gears  
6. Hatch covers and types  
7. Systems on RO-RO ships; ramps, covers, and vehicle lifts  
8. Bow thruster  
9. Cargo pumps and types  
10. Cargo pumps in LNG, LPG, chemical, and oil tankers  
11. Cargo handling system components  
12. Pump applications on tankers, power, and efficiency  
13. Maintenance, troubles, and remedies of cargo pumps  
14. Safety measures on cargo handling and maintenance
