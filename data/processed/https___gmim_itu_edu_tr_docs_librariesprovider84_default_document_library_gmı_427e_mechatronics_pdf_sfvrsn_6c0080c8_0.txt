**Title:** GMI 427E Mechatronics  
**Published Time:** 2022-05-15  

**Course Name:** Mechatronics  
**Course Code:** GMI 427E  
**Semester:** 5-7-8  
**Local Credits:** 2  
**ECTS Credits:** 2  
**Course Language:** English  
**Course Type:** Elective  
**Course Prerequisites:** None  

**Course Description:**  
Mechatronic systems. Mechatronic system components; sensors, actuators, DAC and ADC. Operational amplifiers. Step and servo motors. DC motors. Signal processing. Programmable systems; PLC, microcontrollers.

**Course Objectives:**  
1. To teach about mechatronics.  
2. To teach about mechatronics systems implemented on ships.  
3. To teach about fault diagnosis and maintenance of mechanical, electrical, electronic components and interfaces.

**Course Learning Outcomes:**  
Students who pass the course will be able to:  
I. Learn about mechatronics systems.  
II. Learn about basic components of mechatronics systems.  
III. Learn about mechatronics systems on board ships and operating principles of them.  
IV. Get knowledge about fault diagnosis and maintenance of mechatronics systems on ships.

**Course Plan:**  
1. Introduction to mechatronics, mechatronics system components  
2. Sensors  
3. Sensors  
4. DAC  
5. ADC  
6. Signal Processing and operational amplifiers  
7. Operational amplifiers  
8. Actuators; relays, solenoids, solenoid valves  
9. Actuators; step and servo motors  
10. DC motors  
11. DC motors  
12. Programmable systems; PLC  
13. Programmable systems; PLC  
14. Programmable systems; microcontrollers
