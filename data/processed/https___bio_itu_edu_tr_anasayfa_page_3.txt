**Department Overview:**
The Molecular Biology and Genetics Department at İTÜ aims to address the significant gap in trained molecular biologists in Turkey. The department focuses on integrating education, research, and technology in molecular biology to ensure that Turkey keeps pace with global advancements in biotechnology and related fields.
