**Title:** Arı Günü: İTÜ Mezunlar Buluşması

**Date:** 5-6 Mayıs 2018

**Event Details:**
- Part of Istanbul Technical University's 245th anniversary celebrations.
- Location: Süleyman Demirel Cultural Center.
- Special plaques awarded to graduates celebrating their 10th, 20th, 30th, 40th, 50th, and 60th anniversaries.
- Activities included a campus tour, kite flying, games and workshops for children, dance performances, music shows, sports club demonstrations, and food and beverage offerings.

**Schedule:**
- **May 5, 2018 (Saturday):** Faculty ceremonies for graduates celebrating 20, 30, and 40 years since graduation.
- **May 6, 2018 (Sunday):** Rectorate ceremony for graduates celebrating 50 and 60 years since graduation (10:00 - 12:00).

**Registration:**
- Graduates advised to contact their respective faculty deans or email mezun@itu.edu.tr.

**Additional Information:**
- Traditional lunch from 12:00 - 14:00.
- Open-air activities from 13:00 - 18:00.
