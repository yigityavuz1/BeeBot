**Title:** Prof. Dr. Mine Yurtsever - Hesaplamalı Kimya ve Biyoloji Grubu

**Author:** Doç. Dr. B. Şebnem Sesalan

**Research Focus:**
- Synthesis and biological examination of macrocyclic compounds (phthalocyanines).
- Investigation of interactions between synthesized compounds and DNA, both theoretically and experimentally.
- Study of antibacterial and antifungal properties in yeast and bacteria.

**Key Points:**
- Design of biologically active macromolecules.
- Analysis of photophysical and photochemical properties post-synthesis.
- Generation of reactive oxygen species using radiation.
- Seeking collaboration with a master's or doctoral student interested in synthesis and biological research.
