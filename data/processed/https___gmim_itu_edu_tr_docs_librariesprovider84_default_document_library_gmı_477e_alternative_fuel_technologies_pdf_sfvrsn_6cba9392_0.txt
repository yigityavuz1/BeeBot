**Title:** GMI 477E Alternative Fuel Technologies

**Published Time:** 2022-05-14

**Course Name:** Alternative Fuel Technologies  
**Code:** GMI 477E  
**Semester:** 7-8  
**Local Credits:** 2  
**ECTS Credits:** 4  
**Course Implementation (Hours/Week):** Theoretical: 2, Tutorial: -, Laboratory: -  

**Department/Program:** Marine Engineering  
**Course Type:** Elective  
**Course Language:** English  
**Course Prerequisites:** None  

**Course Description:**  
- Shipping emissions and emission formation reactions.  
- Maritime conventions and strategies for air pollution prevention.  
- IGF Code and classification societies’ guidelines.  
- Physical, chemical, and special properties of liquid and gas alternative marine fuels and their production methods.  
- Onboard storage, line specifications, combustion concepts, and fuel operations of alternative fuels.  

**Course Objectives:**  
1. To teach physical and chemical properties of alternative marine fuels.  
2. To teach alternative fuel technologies and systems.  
3. To teach combustion concepts of alternative marine fuels.  

**Course Learning Outcomes:**  
Students who pass the course will be able to:  
I. Learn shipboard emissions, emission formation reactions, and maritime conventions on air pollution prevention and alternative marine fuels.  
II. Learn physical and chemical properties of alternative marine fuels.  
III. Understand alternative marine fuel technologies and alternative fuel operations.  
IV. Learn combustion concepts of alternative marine fuels.  

**Course Plan:**  
1. Shipping emissions and emission formation reactions  
2. Maritime conventions and strategies for air pollution prevention  
3. IGF Code and classification societies’ guidelines for alternative fuelled ships  
4. Physical, chemical, and special properties of liquid alternative marine fuels  
5. Physical, chemical, and special properties of gaseous alternative marine fuels  
6. Production processes and methods of alternative marine fuels  
7. Onboard storage of liquid and gaseous alternative marine fuels  
8. Fuel supply system specifications of alternative marine fuels  
9. Combustion at conventional marine diesel engines  
10. Combustion concepts for alternative marine fuels  
11. Onboard alternative fuel operations and bunkering  
12. Preventing adverse effects of alternative marine fuels and effective maintenance  
13. Future alternative fuel and renewable energy combinations for maritime transportation
