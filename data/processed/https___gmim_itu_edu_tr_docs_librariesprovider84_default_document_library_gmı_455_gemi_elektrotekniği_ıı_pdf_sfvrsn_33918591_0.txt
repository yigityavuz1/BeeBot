**Title:** GMI 455 Gemi Elektrotekniği II (Marine Electrotechnics II)

**Course Information:**
- **Course Code:** GMI 455
- **Semester:** 7
- **Local Credits:** 1.5
- **ECTS Credits:** 2
- **Course Type:** Compulsory
- **Course Language:** Turkish
- **Prerequisites:** None

**Course Description:**
- Basic electrical concepts and electrical circuit diagrams.
- Batteries, transformers, ship electrical power generation and distribution.
- High voltage systems, DC and AC generators and motors.
- Motor control systems and circuits, measurement, testing, and fault diagnosis of electric devices.

**Course Objectives:**
1. Teach the fundamentals of electrical machines.
2. Teach electrical systems and devices on board ships.
3. Teach control systems of electrical machines.
4. Set up electrical control systems and carry out testing and measurements.

**Course Learning Outcomes:**
Students who pass the course will be able to:
I. Learn about fundamentals of electrical machines.
II. Learn about electrical systems on board ships.
III. Learn about control systems of electrical machines.
IV. Set up control systems and make measurements.

**Course Plan:**
1. Basic electrical concepts and electrical diagrams
2. Batteries
3. Transformers, ship electrical power generation and distribution systems
4. High voltage systems
5. High voltage equipment insulation and switching circuits
6. DC and AC generators
7. DC Motors
8. AC Motors
9. Motor control devices
10. Motor control circuits
11. Lab: Introduction of electric machinery, measuring equipment, reading electrical circuits
12. Lab: Introduction of control circuit elements, forward running of motor, fault diagnosis and troubleshooting
13. Lab: Reverse running of motor, fault diagnosis and troubleshooting

**Textbook:**
1. Fahrettin Küçükşahin, Gemi Elektroteknolojisi, Birsen Yayınevi, 2010.
2. Christopher Lavers, et al., Basic Electrotechnology for Marine Engineers, Tomas Reed Publication, 2013.

**Assessment Criteria:**
- Midterm Exams: 35%
- Laboratory Work: 15%
- Final Exam: 50%
