**About:**
The ITU Rectorate Bursars and Dormitories Coordination provides accommodation services with a capacity of 5300 beds across Ayazağa, Gümüşsuyu, and Tuzla campuses. Additionally, various levels of dormitory, meal, and financial support are provided to students through scholarships supported by ITU Foundations and private individuals or institutions. In the 2023-2024 academic year, a total of 140 Million TL was provided to students.
