**Research Areas of Berna Doğan:**

- **Drug Discovery and Design using Computational Modeling Methods:**
  - Ligand-based: QSAR, pharmacophore modeling
  - Structure-based: molecular docking, molecular dynamics (MD)
  - Virtual screenings
  - Drug repurposing

- **Conformational Analysis of Protein Structures:**
  - Protein-ligand and protein-protein interactions
  - Construction and validation of homology models

- **Development of Machine Learning Models:**
  - Prediction of molecule selectivity using deep learning
  - Integration of docking, MD, and machine learning – active learning
