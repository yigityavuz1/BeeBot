**Title:** ITU Maritime Cognitive Ergonomics Research Laboratory

**Overview:**
The ITU Maritime Cognitive Ergonomics Research Laboratory specializes in human factors, ergonomics, and cognitive research for the maritime sector. The laboratory focuses on developing innovative solutions that enhance safety, efficiency, and sustainability while fostering collaboration between academia and industry.

**Key Areas of Focus:**
- **Technological Research and Innovative Solutions:** Development of unique solutions tailored to the maritime industry's needs using modern analytical methods.
- **Academic Excellence and Collaborations:** Support for knowledge creation through national and international academic publications and industrial collaborations.
- **Training, Simulation, and Industrial Applications:** Guidance for industry professionals using modern simulation systems and training materials to promote sustainable and safe operations.

**Mission:**
To innovate for safer and more efficient maritime operations by bridging the gap between theory and practice in the maritime sector.
