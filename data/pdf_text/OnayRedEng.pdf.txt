In case your residence permit application is approved:

- Your PTT cargo tracking number will be sent to the phone number you entered during the residence permit application on the E-ikamet website.
- Your residence permit card will be sent to your address in the "Main Address and Contact Information for Staying in Turkey" field that you specified on the second page of the Residence Permit Application form.
- With your PTT Cargo tracking number, you can inquire about the shipping stage of your residence permit card. Your card cannot be delivered to anyone other than you.
- After receiving your residence permit card, you must inform the University about your residence permit information.

In case your residence permit application is rejected:

- You can get information from Presidency of Migration Management centers or by calling 157.
